Paint

Car was painted with Ford America Shelby Acapulco Blue Metallic. This paint has been used from 1967 until 1970, mainly for Shelby variants. In 1970 it has been used by Shelby only. Ford paint code is M3077.

Speedometer

Speedometer is an original 1970 Speedometer with red high beam indicator and odometer. It is mechanically driven by a Speedhut SpeedBox GPS Receiver and therefore shows correct speeds without any connection to the transmission. Gauge face is been converted from mph to km/h.

Gauges

Two additional gauges have been added in the center dashboard slot, where usually the A/C would be. These are AutoMeter 2” RPM & Water Temperature gauges.
